Interesting facts about Camel

  1. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  2. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.
  3. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.

Insteresting facts about Chameleon

  1. Chameleons have eyes that can swivel. They can look at two different things at once.
  2. The panther chameleon changes its color to yellow or red when it’s angry.
  3. When a chameleon is cold, it will become darker in color. Dark colors attract heat better than light ones.

Insteresting facts about Pegion

  1. Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.
  2. Pigeons are highly sociable animals. They will often be seen in flocks of 20-30 birds.
  3. Pigeons can fly at altitudes up to and beyond 6000 feet, and at an average speed of 77.6 mph.
    The fastest recorded speed is 92.5 mph.
